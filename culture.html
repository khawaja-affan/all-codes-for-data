Culture of Pakistan

Traditions

Pakistan’s culture is rich with traditions from Islamic values, family importance, and hospitality. Respect for elders and love for food are deeply rooted in society.

Festivals

Major festivals include Eid-ul-Fitr, Eid-ul-Adha, Independence Day on August 14, and cultural events like Basant and Urs (Sufi festivals).

Traditional Clothing

The national dress is shalwar kameez for both men and women. Each province has its own cultural attire and embroidery styles that reflect local heritage.
