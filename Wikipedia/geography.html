Geography of Pakistan

Location

Pakistan is located in South Asia, bordered by India to the east, Afghanistan and Iran to the west, China to the north, and the Arabian Sea to the south.

Regions

Pakistan has four main provinces: Punjab, Sindh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, and Balochistan. It also includes Azad Kashmir and Gilgit-Baltistan.

Climate

The country has a diverse climate – arid in the south, temperate in the north, and alpine in the mountainous regions. Summers are hot and winters are cold, especially in the north.
