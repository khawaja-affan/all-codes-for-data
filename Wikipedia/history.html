History of Pakistan

1947 – Founding of Pakistan

Pakistan was founded on 14 August 1947 under the leadership of Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah. It emerged as a separate homeland for Muslims after the partition of British India.

Major Wars with India

  • 1948 – First war over Kashmir
  • 1965 – Full-scale war, ended in ceasefire
  • 1971 – Resulted in Bangladesh’s independence
  • 1999 – Kargil conflict

Present-Day Pakistan

Today, Pakistan is a nuclear power with significant strategic importance. Despite political and economic challenges, the country is progressing in technology, defense, and education sectors.
